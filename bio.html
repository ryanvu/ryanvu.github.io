RYAN VU

Born and raised in Mississauga, Ontario, Ryan is a young artist who is
visually and auditorily pleased from the beauty of the world we live in.

I created this website as a platform to log my life with my photography,
whether it's in my everyday life or in my travels, I want to share it with the world.

All my photos are shot with my Canon 70D, and edited in Lightroom 5.
All photos are captured and edited by me.
